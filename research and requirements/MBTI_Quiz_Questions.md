## MBTI Dimensions Explained

The quiz measures 4 dimensions:

1. **Extraversion (E) vs. Introversion (I)**
   - E: Gains energy from interaction and external world
   - I: Gains energy from reflection and inner world

2. **Sensing (S) vs. Intuition (N)**
   - S: Focuses on concrete facts and what is present
   - N: Focuses on patterns, possibilities, and future

3. **Thinking (T) vs. Feeling (F)**
   - T: Decides based on logic and objective criteria
   - F: Decides based on values and personal impact

4. **Judging (J) vs. Perceiving (P)**
   - J: Prefers structure and closure
   - P: Prefers flexibility and open-endedness

The jilecek.cz quiz is created by **Jan Jilecek**, a Czech programmer and psychology enthusiast. This version features **adaptive questioning**, where:
